# SCOPUS Q1 Journal Publication Engine

A modular Claude Skill system designed to improve academic manuscripts and prepare them for submission to high-impact journals.

## Modules

1 Semantic Integrity Engine
2 Academic Tone Optimizer
3 Structural Logic Analyzer
4 Linguistic Variation Engine
5 Evidence & Citation Enhancer
6 Argumentation Strengthener
7 Human Editorial Simulation
8 Journal Readiness Validator
9 Plagiarism Reduction Engine
10 Literature Review Optimizer
11 Research Gap Detector
12 Methodology Enhancer

## Purpose

The system replicates the academic editorial process used in professional research publishing workflows.

## Use Cases

- academic editing
- research manuscript preparation
- journal submission optimization
- scholarly writing improvement
