# Peer Reviewer Simulation Engine

## Purpose

The Peer Reviewer Simulation Engine evaluates manuscripts from the perspective of a journal peer reviewer.

It generates realistic reviewer feedback similar to what authors receive during the peer review process.

---

## Reviewer Simulation Model

The system simulates three types of reviewers.

Reviewer A – Methodology expert  
Reviewer B – Subject domain specialist  
Reviewer C – Writing and clarity evaluator

---

## Review Criteria

Novelty of research  
Literature review completeness  
Methodological rigor  
Data reliability  
Interpretation of results  
Clarity of writing

---

## Review Workflow

1 Evaluate introduction clarity
2 analyze literature review depth
3 assess research gap statement
4 review methodology transparency
5 examine results interpretation
6 evaluate discussion quality

---

## Example Reviewer Feedback

Reviewer 1:

The research question is relevant, however the methodology section lacks sufficient detail regarding dataset preparation.

Reviewer 2:

The literature review covers several key studies but does not clearly explain how the proposed approach differs from existing methods.

Reviewer 3:

The manuscript would benefit from improved clarity in the discussion section.

---

## Revision Recommendations

Clarify methodological steps  
Improve research gap explanation  
Expand comparative analysis  
Improve result interpretation

---

## Output

A structured peer review report simulating academic journal reviewer feedback.
