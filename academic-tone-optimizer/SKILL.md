# Academic Tone Optimizer

## Purpose

Transforms informal, conversational, or AI-generated phrasing into formal academic language appropriate for journal publications.

## Objectives

- Improve scholarly tone
- Replace casual expressions
- Strengthen formal language structures
- Maintain discipline-specific vocabulary

## Tone Transformation Rules

Replace informal phrasing:

Examples

Instead of:
"This paper talks about"

Use:
"This paper examines"

Instead of:
"We tried to check"

Use:
"This study evaluates"

## Linguistic Standards

- Prefer formal verbs
- Avoid contractions
- Maintain objective tone
- Use neutral academic phrasing
- Avoid overly promotional language

## Structural Adjustments

- Convert conversational sentences into analytical statements
- Improve transition phrases
- Remove conversational fillers

## Target Tone

Writing style consistent with publications from major academic publishers such as Elsevier and Springer Nature.

## Output

Manuscript rewritten with consistent academic tone suitable for scholarly journals.
