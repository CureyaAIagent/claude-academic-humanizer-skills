# Linguistic Variation Engine

## Purpose

Improves stylistic diversity and reduces repetitive sentence structures commonly found in automated text.

## Core Goals

- Increase sentence variation
- Improve lexical diversity
- Reduce repetitive phrasing
- Improve paragraph rhythm

## Detection Mechanisms

The engine analyzes:

- Sentence length distribution
- Lexical diversity
- Phrase repetition
- Clause structure patterns

## Editing Strategy

When repetitive structures are detected:

- Vary sentence lengths
- Introduce subordinate clauses
- Replace repetitive vocabulary
- Restructure sentences

## Example

Original:

"The model performs well. The model is accurate. The model works efficiently."

Optimized:

"The proposed model demonstrates strong performance and achieves high accuracy across multiple evaluation metrics."

## Linguistic Targets

- Balanced sentence complexity
- Improved narrative flow
- Reduced syntactic repetition

## Output

Text with improved stylistic richness and natural writing variation.
