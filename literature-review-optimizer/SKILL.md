# Literature Review Optimizer

## Purpose

Strengthens the literature review section by improving synthesis, organization, and critical analysis of prior research studies.

## Key Responsibilities

- Organize studies by research themes
- Improve the research narrative
- Strengthen synthesis of literature
- Eliminate simple listing of studies without analysis

## Optimization Process

1. Identify key research themes  
2. Group related studies under thematic categories  
3. Compare methodologies used in previous studies  
4. Highlight similarities, differences, and research gaps  

## Analytical Framework

Instead of listing studies individually:

Author A (2021)  
Author B (2022)

Transform into a synthesized analytical structure:

Previous studies demonstrate three major research directions:

1. Predictive modelling  
2. Experimental validation  
3. Comparative evaluation  

## Output

A structured and analytical literature review that demonstrates a comprehensive understanding of the research field and clearly positions the current study within existing scholarship.
