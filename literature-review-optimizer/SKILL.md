# Literature Review Optimizer

## Purpose

Strengthens the literature review by improving synthesis, organization, and critical analysis of prior research.

## Key Responsibilities

- organize studies by themes
- improve research narrative
- strengthen synthesis of literature
- remove simple listing of studies

## Optimization Process

1 Identify key research themes
2 Group related studies
3 Compare methodologies
4 Highlight similarities and differences

## Analytical Framework

Instead of listing studies:

Author A (2021)
Author B (2022)

Transform into:

Previous studies demonstrate three major research directions:

1 predictive modelling
2 experimental validation
3 comparative evaluation

## Output

A structured, analytical literature review demonstrating deep understanding of the research field.
