# Human Editorial Simulation

## Purpose

Simulates the revision process performed by professional academic editors to enhance the clarity, conciseness, and overall readability of academic manuscripts.

## Editorial Focus Areas

- Clarity
- Conciseness
- Coherence
- Readability
- Logical transitions

## Editing Principles

Professional editors typically:

- Remove redundant phrases
- Simplify complex sentences
- Improve transitions between ideas
- Enhance clarity and readability

## Common Editing Transformations

Replace vague expressions with precise academic phrasing.

### Example

Instead of:

"very important results"

Use:

"statistically significant results"

## Editing Workflow

1. Identify verbose or redundant sentences
2. Simplify wording while preserving meaning
3. Improve paragraph cohesion
4. Strengthen transitions between ideas

## Output

Manuscript revised to align with professional academic editorial standards, improving clarity, coherence, and readability suitable for scholarly publication.
