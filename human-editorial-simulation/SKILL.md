# Human Editorial Simulation

## Purpose

Simulates the revision process performed by professional academic editors.

## Editorial Focus Areas

- clarity
- conciseness
- coherence
- readability
- logical transitions

## Editing Principles

Professional editors typically:

- remove redundant phrases
- simplify complex sentences
- improve transitions
- enhance clarity

## Common Editing Transformations

Replace vague expressions:

"very important results"

With precise academic phrasing:

"statistically significant results"

## Editing Workflow

1. Identify verbose sentences
2. Simplify wording
3. Improve paragraph cohesion
4. Strengthen transitions between ideas

## Output

Manuscript revised to match professional academic editorial standards.
