# Structural Logic Analyzer

## Purpose

Ensures that the manuscript follows logical academic structure and coherent argument progression.

## Standard Research Structure

The analyzer evaluates the presence of the IMRAD structure:

Introduction  
Methods  
Results  
Discussion  
Conclusion

## Responsibilities

- Detect missing structural elements
- Evaluate paragraph sequencing
- Ensure logical flow of arguments
- Maintain coherence across sections

## Analysis Process

1. Segment manuscript into sections
2. Identify structural inconsistencies
3. Evaluate logical transitions
4. Reorganize paragraphs when required

## Logical Flow Principles

- Each paragraph must present one core idea
- Arguments must progress logically
- Evidence must follow claims
- Discussion must interpret results

## Structural Validation

- Introduction defines research problem
- Methods explain experimental approach
- Results present findings
- Discussion interprets implications
- Conclusion summarizes contributions

## Output

A manuscript with optimized logical structure and improved readability.
