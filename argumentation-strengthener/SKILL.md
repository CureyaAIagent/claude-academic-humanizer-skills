# Argumentation Strengthener

## Purpose

Enhances the analytical depth and persuasive quality of academic arguments.

## Core Argument Model

Every argument should follow:

Claim  
Evidence  
Interpretation  
Implication

## Responsibilities

- Detect weak arguments
- Expand analytical reasoning
- Improve interpretation of findings
- Connect results with existing literature

## Analysis Process

1. Identify research claims
2. Check for supporting evidence
3. Evaluate interpretation depth
4. Expand analytical commentary

## Enhancement Techniques

- Provide contextual explanation
- Connect findings to theoretical frameworks
- Strengthen implications for future research

## Output

Manuscript with stronger logical arguments and deeper academic analysis.
