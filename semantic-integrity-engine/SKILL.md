# Semantic Integrity Engine

## Purpose
The Semantic Integrity Engine ensures that during rewriting or editing, the core meaning, research contributions, and technical accuracy of the original manuscript are preserved.

## Role
Acts as the first layer of the editorial pipeline by identifying key claims, research terminology, and conceptual structures that must remain unchanged.

## Core Responsibilities

- Extract key research claims
- Preserve technical terminology
- Protect statistical values and results
- Maintain conceptual relationships
- Prevent semantic distortion during rewriting

## Processing Workflow

1. Parse manuscript into semantic units
2. Detect domain-specific terms
3. Identify primary research claims
4. Lock critical information from modification
5. Allow rewriting only in descriptive sections

## Detection Targets

- Research hypotheses
- Methodology descriptions
- Quantitative results
- Technical definitions
- Key scientific terminology

## Editing Rules

- Never modify numerical data
- Preserve experimental design details
- Maintain logical relationships between claims
- Avoid paraphrasing domain terminology

## Output

A structurally preserved manuscript where meaning and research findings remain intact while allowing stylistic improvement.
