---
name: citation-impact-optimizer
description: >
  Academic citation optimization engine that improves research discoverability,
  strengthens literature integration, clarifies conceptual contributions,
  and increases citation potential of research manuscripts.

version: "1.0"
author: CUREEYA

entry_point: system

capabilities:
  - Research Visibility Optimization
  - Literature Integration Enhancement
  - Citation Potential Evaluation
  - Keyword Optimization
  - Conceptual Contribution Strengthening
  - Research Gap Identification
  - Methodology Positioning

objectives:
  - Improve discoverability
  - Enhance literature integration
  - Strengthen conceptual contribution
  - Increase relevance for future research

citation_optimization_strategy:
  - Clarify research contributions
  - Highlight methodological innovations
  - Strengthen theoretical implications
  - Improve keyword relevance
  - Align research with emerging literature trends

high_citation_potential_indicators:
  - Clear research gap
  - Broad applicability
  - Replicable methodology
  - Strong theoretical contribution
  - Clear practical implications

literature_integration:

  purpose: >
    Improve how prior studies are connected to the manuscript
    to create stronger research positioning.

  weak_example: >
    Several studies have examined this topic.

  improved_example: >
    Recent research has examined predictive modelling techniques in
    healthcare analytics, highlighting limitations in cross-dataset
    generalization.

keyword_optimization:

  purpose: >
    Improve research discoverability by refining keywords used
    in the manuscript.

  example:

    original_keywords:
      - AI
      - healthcare

    improved_keywords:
      - machine learning diagnostics
      - clinical decision support systems
      - medical imaging analysis

impact_evaluation:

  evaluation_criteria:
    - Research relevance
    - Methodological novelty
    - Interdisciplinary applicability
    - Clarity of contributions

scoring_model:

  research_visibility_score: "0-100"
  literature_integration_strength: "0-100"
  contribution_clarity: "0-100"
  citation_potential: "Low | Moderate | High | Very High"

workflow:

  step_1: Identify research gap clarity
  step_2: Evaluate literature positioning
  step_3: Analyze contribution clarity
  step_4: Optimize keyword discoverability
  step_5: Evaluate interdisciplinary relevance
  step_6: Generate citation impact report
---

output:

  report_name: Citation Impact Report

  sections:
    - Research Visibility Score
    - Literature Integration Strength
    - Contribution Clarity
    - Citation Potential Assessment
    - Keyword Optimization Recommendations
    - Literature Positioning Improvements
    - Citation Strategy Suggestions
