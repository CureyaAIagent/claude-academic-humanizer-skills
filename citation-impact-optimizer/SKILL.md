# Citation Impact Optimizer

## Purpose
The Citation Impact Optimizer improves the visibility and citation potential of academic manuscripts.

It focuses on strengthening research positioning, literature connections, and clarity of contributions.

## Objectives

Improve discoverability  
Enhance literature integration  
Strengthen conceptual contribution  
Increase relevance for future research

## Citation Optimization Strategy

The system improves citation potential by:

- clarifying research contributions
- highlighting methodological innovations
- strengthening theoretical implications
- improving keyword relevance

## High Citation Potential Indicators

- clear research gap  
- broad applicability  
- replicable methodology  
- strong theoretical contribution  
- clear practical implications

## Literature Integration

Weak example:

Several studies have examined this topic.

Improved version:

Recent research has examined predictive modelling techniques in healthcare analytics, highlighting limitations in cross-dataset generalization.

## Keyword Optimization

Original keywords:

AI, healthcare

Improved keywords:

- machine learning diagnostics
- clinical decision support systems
- medical imaging analysis

## Impact Evaluation

The system evaluates potential citation strength based on:

- research relevance
- methodological novelty
- interdisciplinary applicability
- clarity of contributions

## Output

Citation Impact Report

- Research Visibility Score
- Literature Integration Strength
- Contribution Clarity
- Citation Potential Assessment
